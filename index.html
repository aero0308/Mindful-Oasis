Meditation Basics

Meditation is something everyone can do, here’s how.

1) Take a seat

Find a calm, quiet spot where you feel peaceful and comfortable.

2) Notice your body

Sit in a stable position—chair, cross-legged, or kneeling—that’s comfortable for long periods.

3) Feel your breath

Follow the sensation of your breath as it goes in and as it goes out.

4) Notice when your mind has wandered

When your mind wanders, gently return attention to your breath.

5) Be kind to your wandering mind

Don’t judge your thoughts; simply notice them and return mindfully.

6) Close with kindness

Gently open your eyes, observe sounds, body sensations, thoughts, emotions, and refocus.That’s it!
